# Privacy Policy for Page Digest

**Last updated:** January 2026

## Overview

Page Digest is a browser extension that extracts article content from web pages and sends it to your chosen AI assistant (ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude) for summarization. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What the extension does:
- Extracts text content from the current webpage when you click the extension icon
- Opens your chosen AI provider (ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude) in a new tab
- Pastes the extracted content for summarization
- Stores your AI provider preference locally in Chrome's sync storage

### What we don't do:
- We don't collect analytics or usage data
- We don't track your browsing history
- We don't store any extracted content
- We don't have servers that receive your data

## Third-Party Services

The extension can send content to one of the following services based on your configuration:
- **ChatGPT** (chatgpt.com) - governed by [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/)
- **Gemini** (gemini.google.com) - governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Claude** (claude.ai) - governed by [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)

## Permissions

The extension requires these permissions:
- **activeTab**: To read content from the current page when you click the extension
- **scripting**: To extract article text from pages
- **storage**: To save your AI provider preference

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this document.
