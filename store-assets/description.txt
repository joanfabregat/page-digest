Summarize any article or webpage with one click using ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.

Page Digest extracts the main content from any webpage and sends it to your preferred AI assistant for instant summarization.

FEATURES
- One-click summarization - just click the icon, no popup needed
- Multiple AI providers: ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude
- Smart content extraction using Mozilla's Readability
- Works on news sites, blogs, documentation, and more
- Automatically submits content to your chosen AI

HOW IT WORKS
1. Navigate to any article or webpage
2. Click the Page Digest icon
3. Your AI assistant opens with the article and generates a summary

CONFIGURATION
Right-click the extension icon and select "Options" to choose your preferred AI:
- ChatGPT (OpenAI)
- Gemini (Google)
- Claude (Anthropic)

PRIVACY FOCUSED
- No data collection or tracking
- No accounts required (beyond your AI provider account)
- All processing happens locally
- Content is only sent to your chosen AI when you click the icon

REQUIREMENTS
- An account with your chosen AI provider (ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude)

Perfect for researchers, students, professionals, or anyone who wants to quickly understand long articles without reading every word.
